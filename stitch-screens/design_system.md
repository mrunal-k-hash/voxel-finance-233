# Design System: Voxel Synthesis
**ID:** assets_691a5a27e7e24de99feb985d10796e02

## Style Guidelines
The design system embodies a premium fintech experience that merges high-utility data visualization with a luxurious, futuristic aesthetic. It is defined by "Precision Minimalism"—a style that prioritizes clarity, performance, and depth through a "Linear-inspired" interface. 

The visual narrative relies on **Glassmorphism** and **Tonal Depth**. Surfaces are treated as semi-transparent layers that interact with light and background blurs, evoking the feel of high-end hardware interfaces. The emotional response is one of absolute control, security, and technological sophistication. The UI should feel like a physical glass instrument: polished, responsive, and substantial.
